HOW IT WORKS

Simple for children.Powerful underneath.

Every game the child plays is secretly collecting data. Every tap, every delay, every mistake is a signal. The child never feels assessed. The AI never stops watching.

01

The Child Plays

Age-based games designed for children from 2 to 12 years old. Every level matches their developmental stage — colorful, engaging, and fun. They never know what is happening beneath the surface.

02

The AI Watches

Every interaction generates data. Response times, error patterns, task completion rates, and engagement duration are collected and analysed in real time by the embedded screening engine.

03

Learning Adapts

Content adjusts automatically based on the child's unique performance profile. A child struggling with reading receives more targeted phonics exercises at exactly the right level.

04

Parents Are Informed

When the AI detects consistent patterns of difficulty, parents and teachers receive a clear, plain-language alert through the dashboard — with recommended next steps.

WHAT WE SCREEN FOR

Three core difficulty domains.

NEURO-LEARN focuses on the three most prevalent and impactful childhood learning difficulties across Nigeria and West Africa.

READING & LITERACY

Dyslexia & Reading Disorders

Detects slow letter recognition, reversal of letters and words, poor reading fluency, and avoidance of reading tasks through letter-matching and word-building games.

ATTENTION & FOCUS

ADHD & Attention Disorders

Identifies short task engagement, high error rates, inconsistent performance, and frequent task-restarting through memory and pattern-completion challenges.

COMMUNICATION & SPEECH

Speech Delays & Language Disorders

Flags delayed verbal response, difficulty following instructions, poor sound recognition, and limited vocabulary through voice-response and sound-matching activities.
